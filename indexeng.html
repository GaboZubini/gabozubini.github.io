About me.

My name is Gabriel, I’m an architect based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Father of a beautiful girl. I earned my architecture degree at Universidad de Buenos Aires and am currently pursuing a postgraduate in BIM. I’ve been doing architectural visualization as a hobby since high school, continually improving over time.

I have over 10 years’ experience with AutoCAD and Revit, having completed certified courses on both. Most of my renders are made on Sketchup and Lumion, but I also utilize SKP+Vray and 3DMax.

Having done freelance during my college years, I then worked as construction manager in public worksites all over the Buenos Aires metropolitan area. Currently, I’m working as a designer at Instituto de la Vivienda de la Ciudad de Buenos Aires.
